OUR MISSION

At Letha we believe in creating quality, handcrafted leather products that benefit both our customers and the environment. Currently we only offer two options for our leather shoes as we believe that you should focus your efforts and ‘do what you do well’. We are looking to expand our range in the near future.

SUSTAINABLE

We care about our environment! The leather we are using is organic and comes from sustainable leather manufactring. Our strict quality process ensures that we are making shoes that can still be made in the future by our grand kids’ grand kids.
